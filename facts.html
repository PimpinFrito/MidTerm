Interesting Facts about Venice

List of 5 facts

Venice is completely built on wooden pillars underwater. Those wooden pillars have remained untouched since the city was initally built. Venice sinks a small amount every year. Many people aren't aware of this and believe it's built on an island.

Less than 60 thousand locals still live in Venice. It's mostly tourists in the city and roaming the streets. Venice is known as the City of Canals.

  1. Venice is reknowned for it's bridges, with 417 and about 70 of them are private.
  2. There are 177 canals in Venice
  3. The first casino in Venice was built in 1638
